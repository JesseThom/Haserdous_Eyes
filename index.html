Policies

Appointment Prep

Please show up to appintment with clean lashes. If any makeup residue or oils remain, your lashes will not adhere properly. Please no buns or pony tails

No Entourage

Please be courteous of your lash specialist and do not bring friends, kids ect.. Unless approved by me

Deposit

A $25 non-refundable deposit must be made to secure your spot. Your deposit will go towards your service

No Show

There will be a $65 fee for a no show. You will have to schedule a new appointment. You will also be required to pay in full prior to next appoitment

Late Policy

Please contact me if you are going to be late. I understand life happens. There is a 10 min grace period on all appointments After 10 minutes there will be a $10 late fee. After 15 min your appointment will be cancelled

Recheduling

If you need to reschedule plese do 24+ hours prior to your appointment

Cancellations
